## ü§ñ Why Physical AI Matters

Humanoid robots are poised to excel in our human-centered world because they:

- **Share our physical form**: Navigate spaces designed for humans
- **Learn from human data**: Abundant training data from human interactions
- **Operate in the real world**: Move beyond digital-only AI systems
- **Understand physical laws**: Gravity, friction, momentum, and more

This represents a **significant transition** from AI models confined to digital environments to **embodied intelligence** that operates in physical space.
